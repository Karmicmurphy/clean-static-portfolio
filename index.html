What I test for

Common AI failure types

This is the practical evaluator work: find the failure, explain it clearly, and repair the prompt or workflow.

Lost ConstraintsThe model ignores the user’s real limits and solves a cleaner fake version of the problem.
Unsupported CertaintyThe model states guesses as facts without source support or qualification.
LoopingThe model repeats the same advice without moving the task forward.
OverexplainingThe model buries the useful answer under unnecessary background and filler.
Source / Assumption ConfusionThe model mixes verified user-provided material with invented assumptions.
Restart BehaviorThe model starts over instead of continuing from the established conversation state.
