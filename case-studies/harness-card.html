Case study

Harness Card

AI conversation repair system for lost constraints, loops, overexplaining, unsupported certainty, source confusion, and restart behavior.

Problem

Consumer AI systems often fail in repeatable ways. They lose the user’s constraints, restart the conversation, overexplain, loop, or present assumptions as facts.

What I built

  • Direct, Mini, and Full repair modes.
  • Failure taxonomy for practical AI reliability testing.
  • Reusable repair prompts that restore constraints and working state.
  • Test scenarios across ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Copilot, and Perplexity consumer/free tiers.

My role

Prompt systems designer, AI reliability tester, workflow documenter.

Skills proven

LLM evaluationprompt repairresponse comparisonfailure labelingAI product QA

What I learned

AI failures are often not random. They repeat. A useful repair does not need to be huge. It needs to restore the user’s constraints, identify the failure, and force the next answer back onto the real task.
