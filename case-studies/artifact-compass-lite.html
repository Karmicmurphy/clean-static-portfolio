Case study

Artifact Compass Lite

Local artifact registry and source relationship demo for AI workflows.

Problem

AI-assisted projects create scattered drafts, prompts, notes, images, handoffs, and source fragments. Without a registry, the work becomes hard to trust or continue.

What I built

  • Static browser registry using sample JSON-style records.
  • Artifact search and detail views.
  • Fields for type, status, source boundary, relationships, and proof value.
  • A simple model for tracking what AI can assist with and what human review controls.

My role

Workflow designer and local-first information organizer.

Skills proven

artifact organizationprovenancesource reviewstatic web appdocumentation

What I learned

A small human-readable registry can prevent AI workflow drift. It does not need a database first. It needs clear status, relationships, boundaries, and review rules.
